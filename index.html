What's Squid Game 2?

"Squid Game" Season 2 is the highly anticipated follow-up to the global hit series that originally debuted on Netflix in 2021.

The first season captured the world's attention with its intense storyline, brutal survival games, and sharp social commentary. The series, created by Hwang Dong-hyuk, follows a group of financially struggling people who are invited to participate in a deadly series of children's games, with the ultimate prize being a large cash reward.

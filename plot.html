*Squid Game* season 2 will continue the story of **Seong Gi-hun (Lee Jung-jae)**, who, after winning the deadly game, grapples with the psychological toll of the experience. Gi-hun returns to the game, this time seeking to uncover its truth and stop it from continuing. The **Front Man (Lee Byung-hun)** will have a deeper backstory, exploring his role in the game and his connection to his brother, Hwang Jun-ho, whose fate was left uncertain in season 1. Season 2 will introduce new, more dangerous games, with higher stakes and possibly global expansion, bringing in international players. The show will explore the **organization behind the games**, revealing more about the leaders and their motivations. The fate of **Hwang Jun-ho (Wi Ha-joon)** will be addressed, and the season will continue to explore **social inequality** and human nature, with new players adding fresh dynamics and perspectives. The plot will combine Gi-hun’s quest for justice, new deadly games, and deeper character exploration, promising more shocking twists.
